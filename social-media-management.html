Everything you need to know about our social media management services - answered clearly.

Social media management is the process of creating, scheduling, publishing, and analyzing content across social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, and YouTube on behalf of a brand or business. It includes strategy development, content creation, community engagement, paid advertising, and performance reporting to help businesses grow their online presence and achieve their marketing goals.
We manage all major social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Pinterest, TikTok, and Google My Business. We also manage emerging platforms based on your target audience. Our team will recommend the most effective platforms for your specific business, industry and target demographics to maximize your return on investment.
Our content creation process starts with a brand onboarding session where we deeply understand your brand voice, values, target audience, and competitors. We then create a monthly content calendar with a strategic mix of educational posts, promotional content, behind-the-scenes, user-generated content, trending content, and seasonal campaigns. All content is original, designed to match your brand identity, and must be approved by you before publishing.
Posting frequency is tailored to each platform and your package. Our Starter plan includes 12 posts per month (3 per week), the Growth plan includes 30 posts per month (1 daily), and our Enterprise plan offers unlimited custom posting schedules. We analyze your audience's peak engagement windows using platform analytics and schedule posts at optimal times to maximize reach and interaction.
Yes! We manage paid social advertising on Meta (Facebook and Instagram Ads), LinkedIn Ads, Twitter Ads, TikTok Ads, and YouTube Ads. Our team handles everything from campaign strategy and audience targeting to creative development, A/B testing, daily bid optimization, and ROAS reporting. We focus on measurable outcomes - leads, sales, and brand awareness - not just clicks and impressions.
We track key performance indicators (KPIs) aligned with your business goals: follower growth rate, organic reach and impressions, engagement rate (likes, comments, shares, saves), click-through rate, website traffic from social channels, lead count, conversion rate, and cost per result for paid campaigns. All metrics are compiled into a clear monthly report with trend analysis and strategic recommendations for the month ahead.
